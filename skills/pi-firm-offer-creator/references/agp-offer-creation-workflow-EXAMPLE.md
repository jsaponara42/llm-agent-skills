# Offer Creation Workflow

## Introduction

Welcome to the Offer Creation Workflow. This resource explains, step-by-step, how to create your offer.

This is fundamental to your business. Take the time to complete this thoroughly.


## Step 1: Niche Definition

**Answer the following questions:**

**Which niche are you in? (be specific)**
- Personal Injury Law lead and case management automation

**Which specific problem do you solve for this niche?**
- Leads don’t convert as often as they should
- Once a lead becomes a client, moving that client through all the necessary steps is very time consuming and requires a lot of manual follow ups

**What are the goals of the members of your niche facing this problem?**
- Convert more leads into quality cases
- Reduce the time and effort needed to service the client once they’re onboarded

**What are the consequences of facing this problem?**
- 65% of PI leads are lost due to delayed or inadequate follow up
- 79% of consumers hire the first attorney who responds
- Leads contacted within one minute are 391% more likely to convert than those contacted later
- Lost leads → lost clients → lost revenue
- Inefficient case management → slower time to settlement → less monthly revenue
- Inefficient case management → artificially capped client capacity → artificially less revenue

**What have they tried to do to solve this problem in the past?**
- Hire paralegals, client development specialists, and outsourced labor

**What is their current situation?**
- Inbound leads converting at about 5-10%
- Messy, manual case management system
- Personally chasing down and following up with leads, otherwise paying people to do it
- More than half of their time spent between client-facing and case management instead of high value legal work
- Marketing dollar goes to waste because leads aren’t converting
- High stress fast moving environment, easy to miss small details

**What is their desired situation?**
- More leads converting without spending more on marketing
- Most time spent doing legal work, not paperwork
- Don’t have to worry about the small details. Trust they’re handled
- Time to become the face of the firm - marketing and becoming locally famous

**What do they truly want?**
- Many truly want to help people get what they deserve from their injuries
- Some want to become nationally famous for their brand
- All want to make more money - financial freedom
- All would prefer not to do the boring admin/lead nurturing work that eats away at what they really enjoy about their work
- Grow their firm into a brand that can live without them, so they can sell
- Be the best recognized and respected PI attorney in their area/state/region/USA

**What do they believe?**
	- They are their clients' best chance at getting a good settlement for their case
	- There is a lot of room for growth
	- Spending on marketing is the only way to increase lead → client conversion
	- They think the only way to handle more volume is with more people

**What do they think?**
	- They think highly of themselves
	- They are smart and ambitious
	- They think they’ve seen everything

**What do you know to be true about their lives that you can use in your messaging?**
	- Many are egotistical
	- They like to be considered as having a good reputation
	- They like to be considered as intelligent
	- They want to be seen as strong and tough for their clients' cause

## Step 2: Outcome Definition

**What is the big promise you intend on keeping? What’s the bold claim?**
- We’ll help Personal Injury Law Firms add $30k in monthly revenue in 2 months

**How can you make this promise tangible? (numbers based)**
- If you bring in 100 leads/month, and close 10% (10 clients) of them at an average of $10k payout / client, a 3% increase to 13 clients will increase revenue by $30k/month

**Where will someone be after you solve their problem?**
- More freedom to hire help
- Greater return on ad spend
- More happy clients making referrals
- Breathing room to invest in other time-saving automations

**How will they feel once they have this outcome?**
- Empowered
- Flexible
- Less stressed

**What is the end state they will be in after working with you?**
- Business will have cash-flow to invest in various growth initiatives:
	- Marketing
	- Hiring
	- Streamlining operations

**What will this mean to them? What doors does it open for them?**
- Options for investing in the business and investing in business growth are expanded

**Why is this outcome important to them?**
- A growing business is a healthy business
- Higher ROAS allows for greater ad spend than the competition

**How does this outcome move them closer to their desired situation?**
- They need more money and time to spend on advertising without compromising their income, or their business. This gives them the flexibility they need to invest in their business

**What will their next desired state be once the first one is achieved?**
- Increase leads from advertising to maximize ROAS

## Step 4: Mechanism Definition

**What are the core components of your product?**
- CRM
- Text or email-based automated outreach
- Training to manage CRM once leads become clients

**How do these core components help achieve the outcome?**
- **CRM:** Aggregates leads and puts them in the top of the funnel
- **Automated outreach:** Follows up with leads to qualify and encourage them to sign with the PI law firm
- **Training:** Ensures client experience is seamless

**List what needs to be done in full detail, in order to achieve the outcome:**
1. Onboard client, collect payment
2. Access client’s current CRM
3. Potentially duplicate CRM if necessary
4. Move all new leads into qualification/categorization system
5. Move all qualified leads into drip campaign system
6. Run drip campaigns until they become clients, opt out, or reach time limit
7. When a lead becomes a client, move them out of the campaign so they stop receiving automated communications

**What are the most important items on this list?**
- Move all new leads into qualification/categorization system
- Move all new leads into drip campaign system
- Run drip campaigns

**What are the least important items on this list?**
- Move leads out of campaign

**Can the least important items be removed, while still achieving the outcome?**
- Yes, but it would look bad on the firm

**Now re-do your list but make each step simple, no more than 6-9 words:**
1. Onboard client, collect payment
2. Get CRM login
3. Duplicate to better CRM if necessary
4. Connect CRM to qualification and categorization tool
5. Connect CRM to drip system
6. Message leads regularly until they become clients or opt out




**Can these steps be understood by a 10 year old? (if not, make them more simple):**
1. Lawyer becomes customer
2. Get password for their contact list
3. Move to a better address book if theirs is messy
4. Connect contact sorting tool
5. Connect automatic message sender
6. Send helpful messages to contacts until something happens

## Step 5: Risk Reversal

**Do you guarantee that if someone follows your mechanism, they will achieve the outcome?**
- Yes, if their firm is big enough (300+ qualified leads/month)

**If yes, build out your guarantee:**
- Will you offer a refund? **Yes**
- Are you willing to go ‘in the red’ if it doesn’t work? **No**
- Will you work for free if it doesn't work? **Yes**
- What could you give them as an apology if it doesn’t work?

**Are you willing to work on a performance basis?**
- Yes

**How are you aligning the incentives?**
- Paid for every lead converted over 75% of their average?

**What incentive do you have to ACTUALLY make sure it works?**
- There’s more work to do within the firm, this is just the first step. We want to create a customer that comes back for more.

> The stronger the guarantee, the more likely the customer is to adhere to the mechanism. Bold guarantees make customers way more likely to do what you tell them to.

If you’re worried about offering risk reversal, just realize it’s a math thing: maybe you end up having to refund 2/10 of your customers, but if having the guarantee meant you were able to sign them in the first place, it’s worthwhile.

**Position your risk reversal:**
> Phrases like ‘pay on results’ or ‘pay on performance’ or ‘we only profit when you do’ or ‘legally bound guarantee’ or ‘no win, no fee’

- We only profit when you close more leads

**What is your guarantee? State it below (the bolder the better):**
> If you don’t close 10 extra leads in the next 90 days, we work for free until you do

## Step 6: Polarisation

**Who can’t you help?**
- Firms with lead flow below 30 leads/month
- Firms that are not driving traffic to collect phone numbers and email addresses

**Who is the outcome not for?**
- Firms that are happy at their current size
- Firms that don’t have capacity to handle more cases

**What mindset would someone need in order for you to NOT work with them?**
	- Someone who doesn’t understand their current financial situation
	- Someone who is not tracking their lead to conversion ratios
	- Someone who doesn’t understand the important difference between leads generated and conversion rate

## Step 7: Pricing

**What is your competition charging for a similar outcome?**
- Lawmatics ($3000/yr DIY)
- Clio ($150/user/month DIY)
- Leaddockett (~$300/month)

**What is the outcome you’re promising worth to someone?**
- $30,000 - $100,000/month

**What are they going to be willing to pay for it?**
- $3,000 - $10,000/month

**At which point does price exceed value? (bad)**
- When the amount of extra leads closed is worth less than the cost of the service
- When the extra closed leads are too much for the firm to handle so they get no value

**What do you think the outcome is worth in monetary terms ($)?**
- $3,000 - $10,000/month

**What are you going to charge?**
- $3,000 - $10,000/month

**If you 2x’d your price, what value would you need to add to justify it?**
- Increase conversion rate by 60%

**If you 5x’d your price, what value would you need to add to justify it?**
- Increase conversion rate by 100%

**If you 10x’d your price, what value would you need to add to justify it?**
- Increase conversion rate by 200%
