## SPECIALIZED OFFER: PERSONAL INJURY LAW FIRMS

### The Guarantee
"10 Client Guarantee"

If you don't close 10 extra leads in the next 90 days, we work for free until you do.

How It Works:
Once we set up the lead nurturing system, we'll track every lead that goes through the process and becomes a client. If our system doesn't directly contribute to producing at least 10 new clients within 90 days, we pause our fees and continue working until it does.


Why This Works:
This is just the first step in modernizing their firm. Creating a successful customer who returns for additional services (case management automation, workflow optimization, etc.) is the long-term goal.

Pricing Strategy: $3,000-$10,000/month depending on firm size and lead volume
